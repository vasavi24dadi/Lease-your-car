**FAQ**:
1. **Q: Do I need to connect my credit card or bank account?**
   A: No. We don't need access to your billing. You just manually input what tools you use and what you pay, and our engine does the math.
2. **Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
   A: We update our engine weekly with the latest official pricing from vendors like Anthropic, OpenAI, GitHub, and Cursor.
3. **Q: How does Credex make money if this is free?**
   A: If your audit shows massive savings opportunities, we'll offer you a free consultation to show how you can buy those exact same tools through Credex at a steep discount. 
4. **Q: What happens to my data?**
   A: If you don't enter your email, nothing. If you want the report saved, we store it securely just to send you the link. We never sell your stack data.
5. **Q: Why shouldn't I just use ChatGPT instead of Claude or vice-versa?**
   A: Our audit actually recommends specific tools based on your *use case*. If you're purely coding, we'll tell you the most cost-effective coding tool, not just the cheapest chatbot.
