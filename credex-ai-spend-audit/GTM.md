# Go-To-Market Strategy

**Target User**: CTOs and VP of Engineering at Series A/B startups (headcount 20–100). These are the folks who have a corporate card attached to GitHub, Anthropic, and OpenAI, but haven't hired a dedicated FinOps person yet. They care about burn rate but don't have time to optimize individual licenses.

**Pre-Intent Context**: What are they doing right before they need this? They are likely reviewing their monthly AWS/Stripe burn, noticing the "OpenAI" or "GitHub" line items creeping up, or seeing their runway shorten slightly on their monthly board update.

**Where They Hang Out**:
- **Slack/Discord**: Eng leaders in "Rands Leadership Slack" (specifically `#engineering-management` or `#startups`), "CTO Craft".
- **X (Twitter)**: Scrolling startup/VC tech X (following folks like Gergely Orosz, Lenny Rachitsky).
- **Subreddits**: `r/SaaS`, `r/Entrepreneur`, `r/devops` (when discussing tooling costs).

**First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)**:
1. **Cold Outbound via LinkedIn/Email**: Scrape Series A/B companies funded in the last 12-18 months. Message the CTO: "Hey [Name], we built a free calculator that finds an average of $800/mo in wasted AI seat licenses (Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT). Takes 30 seconds to run. Let me know if you want the link."
2. **"Show HN" on Hacker News**: Position it as an open-source/free utility: "Show HN: We built a Mint for AI Tool Spend to stop startups from overpaying."
3. **Engineering Communities**: Post in Rands Leadership `#shameless-promotion` with a genuine narrative about why we built it.

**The Unfair Distribution Channel**:
Credex's existing network of discounted AI credits. We can guarantee users a cheaper price on the *same tools* they are already using, which nobody else can offer. It's not just "you should downgrade"; it's "we can give you the exact same Enterprise plan for 30% less, click here."

**Week-1 Traction Metrics (If Successful)**:
- 500 Unique Visitors
- 150 Audits Completed (30% conversion from visit to completion)
- 30 Emails Captured (20% conversion from audit to lead capture)
- 5 Consultations Booked for >$500 savings.
